FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, direct answers regarding our materials, timelines, planning permissions, and custom warranties.

What types of materials do you use?

We partner only with premium grade, ISI-certified manufacturers for steel (such as Tata Tiscon or JSW), cement (Ultratech/Ambuja), and premium electrical and plumbing conduits to ensure long-term structure integrity and quality.

Do you provide structural warranties?

Yes, we provide up to 10 years of structural warranty and 1 year of post-handover maintenance warranty covering leakage, seepage, or piping glitches, so you can inhabit your new space with complete confidence.

How do you track project progress?

We utilize digital construction planning tools and share weekly milestone reports, site photo logs, and material supply lists directly with our clients via email/WhatsApp, maintaining absolute transparency.

Are custom alterations allowed during building?

Yes, minor design updates can be incorporated. For major layout modifications, we draft a change request order showing cost adjustments and time extensions for your approval before proceeding.
