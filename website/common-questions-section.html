Common Questions

Plan With Confidence

From timelines and permits to pricing and project changes, here are the answers that help you start your construction journey with clarity.

How long does a project take?

Most projects depend on scope, approvals, and material selection. A custom residential build typically takes 10 to 14 months, while commercial renovations can range from 3 to 8 months. During the first consultation, we share a milestone-based roadmap.

Do you provide fixed-price contracts?

Yes, for clearly defined scopes. We review drawings, materials, timelines, and site conditions before finalizing the estimate. This keeps costs transparent and helps protect you from unexpected budget changes.

Who handles the building permits?

Our team manages the permit process. We prepare documentation, submit applications, and coordinate with local authorities so the project follows regulations and avoids unnecessary administrative delays.

Can I provide my architectural plans?

Absolutely. If you already have architectural plans, we review them for construction requirements, safety standards, materials, and timeline alignment before moving into execution.

What happens if there is a change?

Every change is reviewed before work continues. We evaluate the impact on budget, timeline, and materials, then share an updated plan for approval so you stay in control throughout the project.
